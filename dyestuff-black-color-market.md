<p><strong>The Dyestuff (Black Color) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Basic Dyes</li><li>Acid Dyes</li><li>Disperse Dyes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dyestuff black color market is segmented into three primary types: basic dyes, acid dyes, and disperse dyes. Basic dyes are cationic, suitable for acrylic fibers and some synthetic fabrics, offering vibrant colors. Acid dyes are anionic, ideal for protein and nylon fibers, providing excellent brightness and wash fastness. Disperse dyes are non-ionic, predominantly used for printing and dyeing polyester and other synthetic fibers, known for their ability to disperse evenly in the dye bath. Each type serves distinct applications in textiles and other industries.</p></p>
