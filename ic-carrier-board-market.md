<p><strong>The IC Carrier Board Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Layer Board</li><li>Dual Layers Board</li><li>Multi Layers Board</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IC Carrier Board market is categorized into three types based on the number of layers: single layer, dual layer, and multi-layer boards. Single layer boards are simplistic and cost-effective, suitable for low-density applications. Dual layer boards provide increased functionality by allowing more complex designs and separating power and signal routes. Multi-layer boards, on the other hand, offer the highest density and performance, accommodating intricate circuitry and high-speed signals, making them ideal for advanced electronic devices and applications.</p></p>
