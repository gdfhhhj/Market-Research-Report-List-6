There are two main types of CGM on the market: Retrospective CGM (r-CGM) and Real-time continuous glucose monitoring (RT-CGM). </p><p>Retrospective CGM involves downloading data from a CGM device after a certain period of time to analyze glucose patterns. </p><p>Real-time CGM provides continuous updates on glucose levels throughout the day, allowing for immediate adjustments to insulin dosing and diet. Both types of CGM play a crucial role in helping individuals with diabetes manage their blood sugar levels effectively.</p></p>
