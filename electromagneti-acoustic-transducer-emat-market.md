<p><ul><li>Lorentz Force</li><li>Magnetostriction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electromagnetic Acoustic Transducer (EMAT) market is primarily categorized into two types: Lorentz Force and Magnetostriction. Lorentz Force EMATs operate by generating ultrasonic waves through the interaction of an electromagnetic field and the conductive material, making them suitable for high-speed inspections. Conversely, Magnetostriction EMATs utilize the magnetostrictive properties of ferromagnetic materials to produce ultrasonic waves, enabling effective inspections of thick materials. Both types are essential for non-destructive testing in industries like aerospace, manufacturing, and energy.</p></p>
