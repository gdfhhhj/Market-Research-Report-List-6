<p><p>The electrodialysis membranes market is categorized into three main types: heterogeneous membranes, homogeneous membranes, and bipolar membranes. Heterogeneous membranes feature varying properties, offering selective ion transport and enhancing separation efficiency. Homogeneous membranes provide uniform structure and consistent performance, ideal for specific ion applications. Bipolar membranes enable water dissociation and facilitate the generation of acids and bases, making them suitable for various industrial processes. Each type serves distinct purposes in water treatment and energy generation, contributing to advancements in membrane technology.</p></p>
