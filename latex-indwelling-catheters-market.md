<p><p>The Latex Indwelling Catheters market is segmented into various types based on their specific medical applications. Indwelling urinary catheters are used for draining urine from the bladder, while indwelling plural catheters assist in managing pleural effusions by removing fluid from the pleural space. Indwelling peritoneal catheters facilitate dialysis by allowing fluid exchange in the abdominal cavity. Lastly, indwelling nephrostomy catheters are employed to drain urine directly from the kidneys when normal urinary flow is obstructed. Each type addresses distinct clinical needs.</p></p>
