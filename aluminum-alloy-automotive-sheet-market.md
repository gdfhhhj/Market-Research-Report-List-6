<p><p>Aluminum alloy automotive sheets come in three main types: cast aluminum, rolled aluminum, and extruded aluminum. Cast aluminum sheets are made by pouring molten aluminum into a mold to create the desired shape. Rolled aluminum sheets are produced by passing aluminum ingots through rollers to achieve the desired thickness. Extruded aluminum sheets are made by forcing heated aluminum through a die to create a specific shape. Each type of aluminum sheet has its own advantages and applications in the automotive industry.</p></p>
