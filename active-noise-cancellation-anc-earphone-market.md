<p><strong>The Active Noise Cancellation ANC Earphone Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Feedforward Active Noise Canceling</li><li>Feedback ANC</li><li>Hybrid ANC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) earphone market is categorized into three main types: Feedforward ANC, Feedback ANC, and Hybrid ANC. Feedforward ANC uses external microphones to detect ambient noise and generate anti-noise signals, effectively blocking sound before it reaches the ear. Feedback ANC employs internal microphones to monitor sound within the ear canal, adjusting the noise-canceling response based on what's heard. Hybrid ANC combines both methods for enhanced performance, providing superior noise cancellation in various environments, appealing to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
