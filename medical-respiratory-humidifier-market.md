<p><strong>The Medical Respiratory Humidifier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Type</li><li>Passive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical respiratory humidifier market is primarily categorized into active and passive types. Active humidifiers use a heating element to warm water and produce moisture, allowing for precise control of humidity levels, often suited for critical care environments. In contrast, passive humidifiers utilize exhaled heat and moisture to condition inspired air, making them more energy-efficient and less complex. Both types are essential for optimizing patient comfort and maintaining respiratory health in various clinical settings.</p></p>
