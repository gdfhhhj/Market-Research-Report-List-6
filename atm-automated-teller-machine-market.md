<p><ul><li>On site</li><li>Off site</li></ul></p>
<p><p>On-site ATMs are those located within or near a financial institution, such as a bank or credit union. These ATMs are typically easily accessible to customers of that specific institution. Off-site ATMs, on the other hand, are located in more remote locations, such as malls, grocery stores, or gas stations. These ATMs are often operated by independent companies and can incur additional fees for non-customers. Both on-site and off-site ATMs provide convenient access to cash for users, but serve different markets.</p></p>
