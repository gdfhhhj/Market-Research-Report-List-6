<p><p>The Automatic Blood Collection System market is divided into two primary types: Fingertip Blood Collection Systems and Venous Blood Collection Systems. Fingertip systems are designed for quick, at-home testing, enabling capillary blood samples to be collected with minimal invasiveness, ideal for glucose monitoring and other rapid tests. In contrast, Venous Blood Collection Systems require trained professionals to obtain blood from veins, ensuring larger volumes for comprehensive laboratory analyses, commonly used in clinical settings for various diagnostic purposes.</p></p>
