<p><p>The ETFE (Ethylene Tetrafluoroethylene) architectural membrane market is categorized into three types: transparent, semi-transparent, and opaque. Transparent ETFE offers maximum light transmission, ideal for structures aiming to maximize natural illumination. Semi-transparent membranes allow filtered light, balancing translucency and privacy, suited for environments needing controlled light levels. Opaque ETFE provides complete blockage of light, commonly used for applications requiring privacy or energy efficiency. Each type serves distinct architectural needs while benefiting from ETFE's lightweight, durable, and weather-resistant properties.</p></p>
