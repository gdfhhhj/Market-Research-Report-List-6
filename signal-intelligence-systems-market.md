Ground-Based Intelligence Systems are stationary systems that intercept and analyze signals from a fixed location. Airborne Signal Intelligence Systems are installed on aircraft to gather intelligence in real-time while in-flight. Naval Vessel-Based Signal Intelligence Systems are deployed on ships to monitor and intercept communications at sea.
