V-FFS machines are designed for filling and sealing products in a vertical format, making them ideal for packaging granular or powdery items. In contrast, H-FFS machines operate horizontally, suitable for bulkier products or pouches requiring a more complex packaging process. Both types enhance efficiency and automation in packaging operations across various industries.</p></p>
