<p><p>Self-balancing motorcycles come in two main types based on their technology: with gyroscope and without gyroscope. With gyroscope self-balancing motorcycles use sensors and a gyroscope to automatically stabilize the bike, making it easier for riders to maintain balance. On the other hand, without gyroscope self-balancing motorcycles rely on advanced software algorithms and sensors to keep the bike upright. Both types are gaining popularity in the market as they provide a safer and more user-friendly riding experience.</p></p>
