<p><p>The large size cooling fan market is primarily segmented into axial fans, centrifugal fans, and other types. Axial fans move air parallel to the axis of the fan, making them ideal for cooling and ventilation in various applications. Centrifugal fans, on the other hand, draw air into the fan and expel it at a right angle, providing high pressure and efficiency for heavy-duty tasks. Other types encompass hybrid and special-purpose fans that cater to niche applications across industries like HVAC, manufacturing, and agriculture.</p></p>
