<p><p>Alnico magnets are categorized into three main types: cast alnico, sintered alnico, and bar alnico. Cast alnico is produced by melting and casting the alloy, resulting in a strong and stable magnet primarily used in sensors and electric guitars. Sintered alnico is made by compacting and sintering the alloy, offering higher magnetic performance and complexity in shapes, suitable for industrial applications. Bar alnico is a specific shape that provides versatility for various uses, including electric motor, guitar pickups, and magnetic assemblies.</p></p>
