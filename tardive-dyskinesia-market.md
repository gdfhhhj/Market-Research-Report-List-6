<p><p>Tardive Dyskinesia market types include Dopamine-Depleting Medications, GABA Receptor Agonist Medications, and Anticholinergic Medications. Dopamine-Depleting Medications work by reducing dopamine levels in the brain, which can help manage symptoms of Tardive Dyskinesia. GABA Receptor Agonist Medications target specific receptors in the brain to help regulate neurotransmitter activity. Anticholinergic Medications block the action of acetylcholine, a neurotransmitter that can contribute to involuntary movements. These medications offer different approaches to managing Tardive Dyskinesia symptoms.</p></p>
