<p><strong>The Temperature Sensitive Resistor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>NTC Type</li><li>PTC Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The temperature-sensitive resistor market is primarily categorized into two types: NTC (Negative Temperature Coefficient) and PTC (Positive Temperature Coefficient) resistors. NTC resistors exhibit decreasing resistance with increasing temperature, making them ideal for temperature sensing and control applications. Conversely, PTC resistors have increasing resistance with rising temperature, which makes them suitable for overcurrent protection and self-regulating heater applications. Both types cater to various sectors, including automotive, industrial, and consumer electronics, each requiring precise temperature monitoring and management solutions.</p></p>
