<p><p>The infrared lens (IR lens) market is primarily divided into two types: prime infrared lenses and zoom infrared lenses. Prime infrared lenses have a fixed focal length, offering high image quality and optimal performance for specific applications, such as thermal imaging and surveillance. In contrast, zoom infrared lenses provide variable focal lengths, allowing for more versatile usage in dynamic environments, making them ideal for applications requiring adjustable field-of-view and enhanced flexibility. Both types cater to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
