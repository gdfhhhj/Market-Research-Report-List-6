<p><p>The PET nuclear medicine imaging market primarily encompasses three types: PET-CT, PET-MRI, and other modalities. PET-CT combines positron emission tomography with computed tomography, providing detailed anatomical and functional imaging, widely used in oncology. PET-MRI merges PET with magnetic resonance imaging, offering superior soft tissue contrast and functional insights, crucial for neurological and oncological assessments. Other modalities may include standalone PET systems or hybrid devices, expanding diagnostic capabilities across various medical fields and enhancing patient care through improved imaging techniques.</p></p>
