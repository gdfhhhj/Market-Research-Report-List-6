<p><p>The fire cabinet market features three distinct types: surface mount, semi-recessed, and fully recessed. Surface mount cabinets are installed directly onto walls, making them easily accessible and visible. Semi-recessed cabinets are partially embedded into the wall, offering a balance between aesthetics and functionality while remaining accessible. Fully recessed cabinets are completely integrated into the wall structure, providing a sleek appearance and saving space. Each type caters to different installation preferences and architectural designs, ensuring efficient fire safety solutions.</p></p>
