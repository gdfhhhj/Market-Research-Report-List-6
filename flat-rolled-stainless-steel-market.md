<p><p>The flat-rolled stainless steel market encompasses various product types, including hot-rolled and cold-rolled coils, strips, sheets, and plates. Hot-rolled products are processed at high temperatures, offering improved workability and lower costs, suitable for industrial applications. Cold-rolled products, produced at room temperature, provide enhanced surface finish and tighter tolerances, ideal for aesthetic and precision components. Coils are typically used in manufacturing, while strips and sheets find applications in construction and automotive sectors. Plates are utilized in heavy-duty applications requiring robust materials.</p></p>
