<p><p>The auto dashboard camera market comprises two main types: single-channel and multi-channel dashboard cameras. Single-channel dashboard cameras record video from one perspective, typically facing forward, capturing essential footage of the road ahead. In contrast, multi-channel dashboard cameras offer multiple recording angles, often including front, rear, and even side views, providing a comprehensive overview of the vehicle's surroundings. This enhanced coverage can improve safety and security, appealing to consumers seeking detailed evidence in the event of incidents or accidents.</p></p>
