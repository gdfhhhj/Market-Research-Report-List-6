<p><p>The Field Install Connector market is segmented into two primary types: Mechanical Type and Fusion Splice Type. Mechanical Type connectors utilize physical alignment and mechanical force to join fibers, making them easy to install and use in various environments. Conversely, Fusion Splice Type connectors involve the precise heating and fusing of glass fibers, providing a more permanent and low-loss connection. Both types cater to different installation needs, offering solutions for telecommunications, data centers, and network expansions.</p></p>
