<p><strong>The Hanging Subsoiler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Wing</li><li>Dual-Wing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hanging subsoilers are divided into two market types: single-wing and dual-wing. Single-wing subsoilers have a single shank attached to the frame, which is suitable for lighter soils and less intense subsoiling needs. Dual-wing subsoilers, on the other hand, have two shanks attached to the frame, providing more stability and efficiency for heavier soils and more challenging subsoiling tasks. Both types of hanging subsoilers are used to break up compacted soil layers and improve water infiltration and root growth in agricultural fields.</p></p>
