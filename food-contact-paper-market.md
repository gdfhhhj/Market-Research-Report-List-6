<p><p>The food contact paper market is primarily divided into two main types: non-poly-coated and poly-coated paper. Non-poly-coated paper is biodegradable and often used for items such as bakery products or sandwiches, providing thermal insulation and moisture resistance without synthetic coatings. In contrast, poly-coated paper features a polyethylene layer, which enhances moisture resistance and durability, making it suitable for packaging oily or wet foods. Each type caters to specific food service needs, balancing performance with environmental considerations.</p></p>
