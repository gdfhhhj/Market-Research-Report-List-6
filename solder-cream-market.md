<p><strong>The Solder Cream Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rosin Based Creams</li><li>Water Soluble Creams</li><li>No-clean Creams</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solder cream is essential in electronic assembly, classified into three main types. Rosin-based creams utilize natural resins, providing excellent wetting and solderability, commonly used in traditional applications. Water-soluble creams allow for easy post-assembly cleaning, making them suitable for high-reliability applications where residues can be problematic. No-clean creams are designed to leave minimal residues, eliminating the need for cleaning and enhancing efficiency in manufacturing processes. Each type offers unique benefits to meet diverse industry requirements.</p></p>
