<p><p>The Low Temp Co-fired Ceramic (LTCC) material market primarily includes two types: glass-ceramic systems and amorphous glass systems. Glass-ceramic systems combine crystalline and glassy components, offering enhanced thermal stability and mechanical strength, suitable for high-frequency applications. Amorphous glass systems, on the other hand, consist of non-crystalline structures, providing flexibility in design and processing with good dielectric properties. Both systems cater to diverse applications in electronics, telecommunications, and sensors, emphasizing miniaturization and performance in compact devices.</p></p>
