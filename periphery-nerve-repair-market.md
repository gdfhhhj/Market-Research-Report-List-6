<p><p>Periphery nerve repair market offers various types of products for nerve regeneration including nerve conduit, nerve wrap, and nerve graft. Nerve conduits are artificial tubes used to bridge the gap between nerve endings. Nerve wraps are protective coverings placed around nerve injuries to facilitate healing. Nerve grafts involve transplanting a section of nerve tissue from one part of the body to another. Each type of product serves a unique purpose in promoting nerve regeneration and repairing damaged peripheral nerves.</p></p>
