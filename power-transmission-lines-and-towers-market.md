<p><p>Power transmission lines and towers market is categorized into three types based on the voltage capacity they can handle - High Tension (HT), Extra High Tension (EHT), and Ultra High Tension (UHT). HT lines operate at voltages ranging from 60 kV to 300 kV, while EHT lines handle voltages between 300 kV and 765 kV. UHT lines are designed for voltages exceeding 765 kV up to 1200 kV. Each type of transmission line and tower is designed to efficiently transmit electricity over long distances while ensuring minimal power loss.</p></p>
