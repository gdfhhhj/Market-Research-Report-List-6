<p><p>The bullet surveillance camera market is primarily divided into two types: analog cameras and IP cameras. Analog cameras utilize traditional coaxial cables for video transmission and are typically more affordable, making them suitable for basic security needs. In contrast, IP cameras transmit digital video over networks, offering higher resolution, remote access, and advanced features like motion detection and cloud storage. The choice between these types largely depends on budget, installation requirements, and desired functionality for surveillance needs.</p></p>
