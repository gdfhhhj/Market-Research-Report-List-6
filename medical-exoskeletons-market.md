<p><p>Medical exoskeletons market can be categorized into two main types: powered exoskeletons and passive exoskeletons. Powered exoskeletons are designed with motorized joints that help augment the user's movement, providing support and assistance during various activities. On the other hand, passive exoskeletons do not have motorized components and rely on mechanical structures to provide support and stability to the user. Each type of exoskeleton offers unique benefits and capabilities, catering to different needs and preferences in the healthcare industry.</p></p>
