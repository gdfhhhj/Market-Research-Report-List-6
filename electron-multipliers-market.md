<p><strong>The Electron Multipliers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Channel Electron Multipliers (CEM)</li><li>Continuous-dynode multipliers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electron multipliers market comprises two main types: Channel Electron Multipliers (CEM) and continuous-dynode multipliers. CEMs are widely used in applications requiring high sensitivity and fast response, as they amplify individual electrons through a series of dynodes. Continuous-dynode multipliers, on the other hand, offer higher gain and are characterized by a continuous metal surface that allows for efficient electron amplification. Both types serve critical roles in fields like mass spectrometry, particle detection, and imaging technologies, driving innovations and improvements in detection efficiency.</p></p>
