<p><strong>The Semiconductor Photoresist Stripping Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Photoresist Stripping</li><li>Negative Photoresist Stripping</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor photoresist stripping market comprises two main types: Positive Photoresist Stripping and Negative Photoresist Stripping. Positive photoresist stripping involves using a chemical solvent to remove the exposed areas of the photoresist layer, leaving behind the unexposed areas. In contrast, negative photoresist stripping requires the removal of the unexposed areas, leaving behind the patterned areas. Both types of photoresist stripping are crucial processes in semiconductor manufacturing to achieve precise and accurate patterns on the semiconductor surface.</p></p>
