<p><p>The Lumbar Artificial Disc market comprises two primary types: metal and biopolymer discs. Metal discs, typically made from materials like titanium or cobalt-chromium, offer durability and strong mechanical support, making them suitable for patients requiring long-term solutions. In contrast, biopolymer discs utilize advanced synthetic materials that mimic natural disc properties, promoting better integration with spinal tissues and reduced rejection risk. Both types aim to alleviate back pain and restore functionality, catering to diverse patient needs and preferences in spinal surgery.</p></p>
