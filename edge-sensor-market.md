<p><strong>The Edge Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laser Edge Sensors</li><li>Ultrasonic Edge Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The edge sensor market is primarily divided into two types: laser edge sensors and ultrasonic edge sensors. Laser edge sensors utilize focused beams of laser light to detect the edges of various materials with high precision, making them ideal for applications requiring accuracy in positioning and measurement. Ultrasonic edge sensors, on the other hand, employ sound waves to detect edges, providing reliable performance in various environments. Both types are essential in automation, robotics, and manufacturing for enhanced efficiency and quality control.</p></p>
