<p><strong>The Egg Protein Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Whole Egg Powder</li><li>Egg White Powder</li><li>Egg Yolk Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Egg protein powder market is categorized into three types: Whole Egg Powder, Egg White Powder, and Egg Yolk Powder. Whole Egg Powder is made by spray-drying liquid egg, retaining both egg white and yolk. Egg White Powder is produced by drying the egg whites specifically. Egg Yolk Powder is made by dehydrating the yolks. Each type of powder offers unique benefits and is used in various food products like baked goods, protein bars, and supplements for their high protein content and nutritional value.</p></p>
