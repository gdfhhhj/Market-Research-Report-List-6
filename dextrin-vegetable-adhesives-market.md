<p><p>Dextrin vegetable adhesives are classified into two main types: white dextrin and yellow dextrin. White dextrin is a water-soluble adhesive that is widely used in paper and textile industries due to its excellent bonding properties. Yellow dextrin, on the other hand, is mainly employed in the construction industry for its strong adhesive strength and fast drying time. Both types of dextrin vegetable adhesives offer environmentally friendly alternatives to traditional synthetic adhesives, making them increasingly popular in various industries.</p></p>
