<p><strong>The Car Seat Ventilation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Blow Ventilation System</li><li>Suction Ventilation System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Car Seat Ventilation System market comprises two primary types: Blow Ventilation System and Suction Ventilation System. The Blow Ventilation System circulates air through perforated seat cushions, providing a cooling effect to enhance passenger comfort during warm weather. In contrast, the Suction Ventilation System draws hot air away from the seat, effectively reducing heat buildup and improving overall seat comfort. Both systems aim to enhance the driving experience by maintaining optimal temperature and increasing user satisfaction in vehicles.</p></p>
