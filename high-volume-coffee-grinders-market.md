<p><p>High volume coffee grinders are available in two main types - roller and burr grinders. Roller grinders use a series of evenly spaced rollers to crush and grind coffee beans to a consistent size, producing a uniform grind. On the other hand, burr grinders use two abrasive surfaces, called burrs, to crush the beans between them. Burr grinders are known for their ability to produce a more precise and customizable grind size, making them popular among coffee enthusiasts and professionals.</p></p>
