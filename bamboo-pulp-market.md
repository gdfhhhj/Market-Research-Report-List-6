<p><p>The bamboo pulp market is categorized mainly into two types: bleached pulp and unbleached pulp. Bleached pulp undergoes a chemical process to achieve a white appearance, enhancing its suitability for high-quality paper products, packaging, and sanitary items. In contrast, unbleached pulp retains its natural color and is often preferred for products that prioritize eco-friendliness and sustainability. Both types are valued for their biodegradability, strength, and low environmental impact, making bamboo pulp a popular choice in the paper industry.</p></p>
