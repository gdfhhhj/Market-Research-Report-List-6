<p><p>The Optical Fibers Planar Lightwave Circuit (PLC) splitter market comprises primarily two types: 1×N and 2×N PLC splitters. The 1×N PLC splitter divides an optical signal from one input fiber into multiple output fibers, making it ideal for applications requiring signal distribution to several endpoints, such as in telecom networks. Conversely, the 2×N PLC splitter has two input fibers and splits the signal to multiple outputs, providing flexibility for scenarios where redundancy and load balancing are essential.</p></p>
