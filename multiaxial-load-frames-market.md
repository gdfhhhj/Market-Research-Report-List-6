<p><p>Multiaxial load frames are essential testing systems used to evaluate materials and components under complex loading conditions. Axial/Torsional Test Systems apply forces along the axis and twist the specimen, assessing its strength and deformation characteristics. Planar Biaxial Test Systems exert loads in two perpendicular directions, simulating real-world scenarios where materials face simultaneous stresses. Both types are crucial for industries such as aerospace, automotive, and civil engineering, ensuring the reliability and safety of materials and structures.</p></p>
