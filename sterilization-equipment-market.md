<p><ul><li>High Temperature Sterilization</li><li>Low Temperature Sterilization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sterilization equipment market is categorized into high-temperature and low-temperature sterilization methods. High-temperature sterilization, often utilizing steam or dry heat, is effective for materials that can withstand elevated temperatures, commonly used in hospitals and laboratories. Low-temperature sterilization, on the other hand, employs methods like ethylene oxide or hydrogen peroxide plasma, suitable for heat-sensitive items, such as medical instruments and devices. Both types serve essential roles in ensuring safety and preventing contamination across various industries, particularly healthcare.</p></p>
