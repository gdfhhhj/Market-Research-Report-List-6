<p><ul><li>Millivolt Thermostats</li><li>24 Volt Thermostats</li><li>Line Voltage Thermostats</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrical room thermostats are categorized into three main types: millivolt thermostats, 24-volt thermostats, and line voltage thermostats. Millivolt thermostats operate on low voltage, typically used with gas-powered heating systems, ensuring efficient temperature control. 24-volt thermostats are commonly found in HVAC systems, providing reliable control for central heating and cooling. Line voltage thermostats measure and regulate higher voltage systems, often used in electric baseboard heaters. Each type serves specific applications, catering to varied heating and cooling requirements.</p></p>
