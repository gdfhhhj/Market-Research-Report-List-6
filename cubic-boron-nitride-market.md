<p><ul><li>CBN Monocrystalline</li><li>CBN Micro Mist</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cubic boron nitride (CBN) market is primarily categorized into two types: CBN monocrystalline and CBN micro mist. CBN monocrystalline consists of single-crystal structures, ideal for high-performance cutting tools due to superior hardness and thermal stability. In contrast, CBN micro mist is a form of ultra-fine CBN particles used in polishing applications and coatings, offering enhanced surface finish and extended tool life. Both types cater to various industrial needs, driving demand in machining and material processing sectors.</p></p>
