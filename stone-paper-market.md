<p><p>The Stone Paper market can be divided into various types based on production methods. RPD (Resin Polyethylene Dupont) involves blending mineral powder with a small amount of non-toxic resin to create a durable yet flexible paper. RBD (Rich Mineral Paper) uses a higher ratio of mineral powder to resin, resulting in a sturdier paper suitable for unique applications. Other markets in the Stone Paper industry may include specialty products such as water-resistant paper or paper with specific print capabilities, catering to different consumer needs.</p></p>
