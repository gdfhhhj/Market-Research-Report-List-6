<p><strong>The Continuous Variable Transmission Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Belt Connection</li><li>Chain Connection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Continuous Variable Transmission (CVT) technologies can be categorized into two main market types: belt connections and chain connections. Belt CVTs utilize a flexible belt and pulley system, offering smooth gear transitions and higher efficiency, making them popular in small vehicles and scooters. In contrast, chain CVTs use a chain mechanism for power transfer, providing robustness and reliability, often favored in more powerful applications like motorcycles and some automotive models. Both types enhance performance and driving experience by optimizing engine output.</p></p>
