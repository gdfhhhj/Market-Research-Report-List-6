<p><strong>The Isolated Current Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open loop</li><li>Closed loop</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Isolated current sensors are categorized into two main market types: open-loop and closed-loop sensors. Open-loop current sensors provide direct measurements of current without feedback, making them simpler and often more cost-effective. In contrast, closed-loop sensors utilize a feedback system for enhanced accuracy and stability, measuring the difference between input and output currents. These sensors are vital for applications requiring precise current measurement while ensuring electrical isolation, making them suitable for industrial, automotive, and renewable energy sectors.</p></p>
