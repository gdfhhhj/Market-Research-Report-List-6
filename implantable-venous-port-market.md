<p><strong>The Implantable Venous Port Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Lumen</li><li>Double-Lumen</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The implantable venous port market is categorized into single-lumen and double-lumen types. Single-lumen ports consist of one chamber, designed for straightforward access to a single vein, facilitating the administration of medications or nutrients. In contrast, double-lumen ports feature two separate chambers, allowing simultaneous access to the bloodstream for multiple therapies, which is particularly beneficial for patients requiring concurrent treatments. This dual capability helps reduce the need for multiple access points, enhancing patient comfort and care efficiency.</p></p>
