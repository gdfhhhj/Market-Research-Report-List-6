<p><p>Medical laser devices market is segmented into four main types based on the technology used: solid-state laser systems, gas laser systems, dye lasers systems, and diode laser systems. Solid-state laser systems use solid materials like crystals or glasses as lasing medium. Gas laser systems use gases like CO2 or argon as the medium. Dye lasers systems utilize liquid dyes as the medium. Diode laser systems use semiconductor diodes to generate laser beams. Each type has unique advantages and applications in medical treatments.</p></p>
