<p><strong>The Gesture Control Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CAN Communication</li><li>LIN Communication</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Gesture Control Module market can be categorized into two main communication types: CAN (Controller Area Network) and LIN (Local Interconnect Network). CAN communication is widely used in automotive applications due to its robustness and ability to allow multiple devices to communicate in real-time over a single bus, making it ideal for complex gesture control systems. On the other hand, LIN communication serves simpler, cost-sensitive applications, offering lower bandwidth and allowing for efficient, low-speed communication tailored for basic gesture functions in vehicles.</p></p>
