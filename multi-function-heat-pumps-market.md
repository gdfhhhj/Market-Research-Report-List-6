<p><strong>The Multi-Function Heat Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>2 Pipes System</li><li>4 Pipes System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multi-function heat pump market is divided into two main systems: the 2-pipe system and the 4-pipe system. The 2-pipe system operates with one pipe for circulating refrigerant for both heating and cooling, making it more cost-effective but limited in simultaneous heating and cooling capabilities. In contrast, the 4-pipe system utilizes separate pipes for heating and cooling, allowing for simultaneous operation. This flexibility caters to diverse climate control needs in residential and commercial applications, enhancing energy efficiency.</p></p>
