<p><p>Spray sunscreen market can be divided into physical sunscreen, chemical sunscreen, and physical and chemical sunscreen. Physical sunscreen uses mineral ingredients like zinc oxide or titanium dioxide to create a barrier on the skin that reflects and scatters UV rays. Chemical sunscreen, on the other hand, absorbs UV rays and converts them into heat. Physical and chemical sunscreen combine both methods for optimal sun protection. Consumers can choose the type of sunscreen based on their preference and skin sensitivity.</p></p>
