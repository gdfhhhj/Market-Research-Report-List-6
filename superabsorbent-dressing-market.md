<p><p>The superabsorbent dressing market is segmented into adherent and non-adherent types. Adherent dressings stick to the wound site, providing secure coverage and minimizing movement, ideal for moderate to heavily exudating wounds. Non-adherent dressings, on the other hand, do not stick to the wound, which helps reduce pain during dressing changes and is suitable for sensitive or fragile skin. Both types are designed to manage moisture effectively and promote optimal healing in various clinical settings.</p></p>
