<p><p>Cold forming machines are categorized based on the number of die stations they utilize, impacting their efficiency and output. A 1-die station is simple and cost-effective for basic parts. The 2-die station allows for more complex shapes, while a 3-die station offers increased versatility and production speed. The 4-die and 5-die stations further enhance capabilities for high-volume manufacturing and intricate designs. Other machines may include specialized configurations tailored to unique industry requirements, optimizing production processes.</p></p>
