<p><strong>The Dental X-ray Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stationary Anode</li><li>Rotating Anode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental X-ray tube market is primarily categorized into stationary anode and rotating anode types. Stationary anode tubes have a fixed target for electron interaction, providing durable performance for standard dental imaging needs. In contrast, rotating anode tubes feature a rotating target that increases heat dissipation, enabling higher exposure times and improved image quality. Both types are essential in dental diagnostics, catering to varying requirements for image clarity, speed, and patient throughput in dental practices.</p></p>
