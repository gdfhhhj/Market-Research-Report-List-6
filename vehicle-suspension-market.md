<p><strong>The Vehicle Suspension Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydraulically Actuated Suspension</li><li>Electronically Actuated Suspension</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vehicle Suspension Market comprises two primary types: hydraulically actuated suspension and electronically actuated suspension. Hydraulically actuated systems utilize fluid pressure to adjust the suspension characteristics, offering improved ride comfort and handling by absorbing shocks effectively. Conversely, electronically actuated suspension employs electronic controls, allowing for real-time adjustments based on road conditions and driving dynamics. This advanced system enhances vehicle stability and performance, providing a more adaptive driving experience. Both types aim to improve safety, comfort, and overall vehicle handling.</p></p>
