<p><p>Pancreatic stents are used to prevent or relieve blockages in the pancreatic duct. There are three main types of pancreatic stents: straight, wedge, and curved. Straight pancreatic stents are often used for straightforward blockages, while wedge stents are designed to anchor in place and provide stability. Curved pancreatic stents are used for more complex ductal anatomy. Each type of stent has its own unique advantages and is chosen based on the specific needs of the patient and the complexity of the blockage.</p></p>
