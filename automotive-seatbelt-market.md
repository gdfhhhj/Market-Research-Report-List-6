<p><strong>The Automotive Seatbelt Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ELR Three Point Seatbelt</li><li>ALR Three Point Seatbelt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive seatbelt market primarily features two types: ELR (Emergency Locking Retractor) three-point seatbelts and ALR (Automatic Locking Retractor) three-point seatbelts. ELR seatbelts automatically adjust to the occupant's movements while locking during sudden stops or crashes, enhancing safety. In contrast, ALR seatbelts lock automatically when pulled out fully, securing child seats or luggage when needed. Both systems provide crucial safety features, ensuring passenger protection during accidents while catering to different usage needs within vehicles.</p></p>
