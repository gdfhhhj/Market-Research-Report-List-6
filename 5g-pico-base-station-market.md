SA architecture operates independently, utilizing a 5G core network for all communications, enabling lower latency and enhanced performance. This dual approach offers flexibility, as NSA facilitates a gradual transition to full 5G capabilities, while SA provides cutting-edge features and efficiency for future-ready connectivity.</p></p>
