<p><strong>The Human Milk Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Near-IR Milk Analyzers</li><li>Mid-IR Milk Analyzers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Human Milk Analyzer market can be categorized into two main types based on their technology: Near-Infrared (Near-IR) Milk Analyzers and Mid-Infrared (Mid-IR) Milk Analyzers. Near-IR analyzers utilize light in the near-infrared spectrum to measure the composition of human milk, providing quick and non-destructive analysis. In contrast, Mid-IR analyzers use mid-infrared light to achieve accurate and detailed assessments of fat, protein, and carbohydrate content in milk, offering a more comprehensive view of nutritional quality. Both technologies cater to healthcare providers and researchers focusing on infant nutrition.</p></p>
