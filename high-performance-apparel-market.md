Cotton provides breathability and comfort but is less effective in moisture management. Wool, particularly merino, is valued for its thermal regulation, natural odor resistance, and moisture-wicking qualities, making it suitable for both active and casual wear. Each material caters to different consumer needs and activity levels.</p></p>
