<p><strong>The Railway Infrastructure Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sub-Structure</li><li>Super Structure</li><li>Special Structure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Railway infrastructure equipment market can be categorized into three main types: sub-structure, super structure, and special structure. Sub-structure equipment includes components like tracks, ballasts, and sleepers, which form the foundation of the railway system. Super structure equipment comprises rails, switches, and crossings that are crucial for the functioning of the tracks. Special structure equipment includes signaling systems, electrification infrastructure, and overhead lines that provide additional functionality and safety measures to the railway network. Each type of equipment plays a vital role in ensuring a smooth and efficient railway system.</p></p>
