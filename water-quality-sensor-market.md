<p><p>Water quality sensor market encompasses various types of sensors to measure different parameters of water quality. Chlorine residual sensor is used to measure the amount of remaining chlorine in water. TOC sensor measures total organic carbon levels. Turbidity sensor measures the cloudiness of water. Conductivity sensor measures the water's ability to conduct electricity. PH sensor measures the acidity or alkalinity of water. ORP sensor measures oxidation-reduction potential. Each sensor type plays a crucial role in monitoring and maintaining water quality for various purposes.</p></p>
