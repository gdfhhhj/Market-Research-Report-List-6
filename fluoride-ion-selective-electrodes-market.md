<p><p>Fluoride ion selective electrodes (FISEs) are critical for measuring fluoride ion concentrations in various applications, including environmental monitoring and water quality assessment. The market is primarily segmented into liquid membrane and PVC membrane types. Liquid membrane electrodes utilize a flexible, organic solvent-based membrane that selectively allows fluoride ions to pass, offering high sensitivity and a fast response. In contrast, PVC membrane electrodes employ a solid polymer matrix, which provides stability and ease of fabrication, making them popular for routine testing.</p></p>
