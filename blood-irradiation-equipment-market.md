<p><ul><li>X-ray Blood Irradiation Equipment</li><li>Gamma-ray Blood Irradiation Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The blood irradiation equipment market is primarily divided into two types: X-ray blood irradiation equipment and gamma-ray blood irradiation equipment. X-ray blood irradiation utilizes high-energy X-rays to effectively irradiate blood products, aiming to eliminate pathogens while preserving blood components. In contrast, gamma-ray blood irradiation employs gamma radiation from isotopes like Cobalt-60, providing deep penetration and efficient sterilization. Both technologies are critical in ensuring the safety of blood transfusions by reducing the risk of transfusion-transmitted infections.</p></p>
