Plasma sterilizers employ hydrogen peroxide vapor to sterilize heat-sensitive devices at lower temperatures. Other sterilization methods may include dry heat or chemical sterilization, catering to specific requirements of certain medical instruments. Each type offers unique advantages depending on the materials being sterilized and the desired efficiency in infection control.</p></p>
