<p><p>Barley market types include Quick Pearl Barley, Pearl Barley, Barley Flour, Barley Flakes, Barley Grits, Hulless Barley, and Hulled Barley. Quick Pearl Barley is partially processed for quicker cooking, while Pearl Barley is fully processed. Barley Flour is ground into powder for baking and cooking. Barley Flakes are flattened grains used in cereals. Barley Grits are coarsely ground for porridge or baking. Hulless Barley has no outer hull, making it easier to process, while Hulled Barley still has its hull intact. Each type offers unique culinary uses and health benefits.</p></p>
