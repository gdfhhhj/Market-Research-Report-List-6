<p><p>The vehicle chassis dynamometer market is divided into two primary types: multi-roller and single roller dynos. Multi-roller chassis dynos use multiple rollers to simulate road conditions more accurately, offering better performance measurement for various vehicle types, particularly high-power applications. Conversely, single roller dynos are simpler and more cost-effective, making them suitable for smaller shops or less demanding testing environments. Each type serves different needs in vehicle performance testing, calibration, and emissions analysis, catering to diverse customer requirements.</p></p>
