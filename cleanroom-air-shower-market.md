<p><p>The Cleanroom Air Shower market can be categorized into systems utilizing HEPA filters and those employing ULPA filters. HEPA filters capture particles down to 0.3 microns with an efficiency of 99.97%, making them suitable for general cleanroom applications. In contrast, ULPA filters offer a higher efficiency, capturing particles as small as 0.12 microns at 99.999% efficiency, ideal for environments requiring stringent contamination control. Both types enhance air quality and reduce particle ingress, crucial for industries like pharmaceuticals and electronics.</p></p>
