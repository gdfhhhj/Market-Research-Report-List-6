<p><p>Fruit concentrates are available in two main types: liquid concentrate and powder concentrate. Liquid concentrates are extracted from fruit juices through a process that removes water content, resulting in a thick liquid form that is easier to handle and store. Powder concentrates, on the other hand, are derived from dehydrated fruit juices and are in powder form, making them convenient for transportation and storage. Both types of concentrates are used in various industries such as food and beverage for flavoring and sweetening purposes.</p></p>
