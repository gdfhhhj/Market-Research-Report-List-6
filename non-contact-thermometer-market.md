<p><p>A non-contact thermometer, also known as an infrared thermometer, is a device that measures temperature from a distance without making any physical contact with the object being measured. It works by detecting the amount of infrared energy emitted by an object and converting it into temperature readings. Non-contact thermometers are widely used in various industries such as healthcare, food industry, automotive, and manufacturing due to their convenience, accuracy, and hygiene benefits.</p><p>The non-contact thermometer market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.2% during the forecast period. Furthermore, the innovation of advanced features such as Bluetooth connectivity, data storage, and user-friendly interfaces in non-contact thermometers is expected to fuel market growth.</p><p>Overall, the non-contact thermometer market is witnessing steady growth due to the increasing adoption of these devices across various industries for temperature measurement and monitoring purposes.</p></p>
