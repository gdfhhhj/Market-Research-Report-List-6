TDI 65/35 has a higher proportion of TDI-2,6, offering enhanced properties for specific applications. TDI 100 is purely the 2,4 isomer, primarily used in specialized applications requiring high reactivity and performance. Each type serves distinct industrial needs in producing foams, coatings, and elastomers.</p></p>
