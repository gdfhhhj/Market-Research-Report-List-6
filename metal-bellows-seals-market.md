<p><p>Metal bellows seals are specialized sealing solutions used in various industrial applications. The market can be divided into two primary types: stationary and rotary seals. Stationary metal bellows seals are designed to create a seal in applications where the parts remain fixed, preventing fluid leakage. In contrast, rotary metal bellows seals are used in systems with rotating components, accommodating movement while maintaining a tight seal. Both types are crucial for enhancing equipment reliability and reducing maintenance needs in demanding environments.</p></p>
