<p><ul><li>Piezo</li><li>Pirani</li><li>CDG</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vacuum pressure sensors market comprises three main types: Piezoelectric, Pirani, and Capacitance Diaphragm Gauge (CDG). Piezoelectric sensors utilize piezoelectric crystals to measure pressure changes through electrical charge, ideal for dynamic pressure applications. Pirani sensors operate based on thermal conductivity variations, making them suitable for measuring low vacuum levels. CDG sensors measure pressure variations through capacitance changes in a diaphragm, effective for high vacuum environments. Each type serves distinct applications across industries, including semiconductor manufacturing and research.</p></p>
