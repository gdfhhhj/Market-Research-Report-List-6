<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type Electrostatic Chuck</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type Electrostatic Chuck</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bipolar electrostatic chucks market is primarily categorized into two types: Coulomb Type and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type electrostatic chucks. Coulomb Type electrostatic chucks utilize an electric field created by charged plates to hold substrates in place, suitable for various applications. In contrast, JR Type chucks employ surface charge modulation, enhancing grip on larger wafers under high temperatures. Both types are essential in semiconductor manufacturing, providing precise control and stability during processes like wafer handling and lithography.</p></p>
