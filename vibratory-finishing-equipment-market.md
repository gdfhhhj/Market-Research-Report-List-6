<p><p>The vibratory finishing equipment market includes three main types: centrifugal, linear, and CNC machines. Centrifugal machines utilize high-speed rotation to enhance the finishing process, allowing for efficient and precise results. Linear vibratory machines employ a straightforward, linear motion to enable effective deburring and polishing, suitable for bulk processing. CNC vibratory finishing systems integrate advanced computer controls for precision and versatility, accommodating complex geometries and tailored finishing requirements. Each type serves specific applications, enhancing surface quality in various industries.</p></p>
