<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Artificial Pancreas Device System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Control-to-Range (CTR) System</li><li>Threshold Suspended Device System</li><li>Control - to - Target (CTT) System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Control-to-Range (CTR) System maintains blood glucose levels within a specified range by automatically adjusting insulin delivery. Threshold Suspended Device System temporarily suspends insulin delivery when glucose levels reach a certain threshold to prevent hypoglycemia. Control-to-Target (CTT) System aims to maintain blood glucose levels close to a specific target by adjusting insulin delivery accordingly. These different types of Artificial Pancreas Device Systems offer personalized and automated management of diabetes, resulting in improved quality of life for patients.</p></p>
