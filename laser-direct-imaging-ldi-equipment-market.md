<p><p>The Laser Direct Imaging (LDI) equipment market is characterized by two primary types: Polygon Mirror 365nm and DMD 405nm. The Polygon Mirror 365nm utilizes a rotating mirror system to direct laser beams for high-resolution imaging on substrates, making it suitable for various applications in printed circuit boards (PCBs) and photomasks. Conversely, the DMD 405nm employs Digital Micromirror Device technology, allowing precise control of individual pixels for enhanced imaging accuracy, commonly used in semiconductor manufacturing and advanced photolithography processes.</p></p>
