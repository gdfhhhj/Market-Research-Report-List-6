<p><p>The soft robot grippers market is primarily categorized into electric and pneumatic grippers. Electric grippers utilize motors and sensors for precise control and adaptability, enabling them to handle delicate objects with care. They are often preferred for applications requiring exact positioning. Pneumatic grippers, on the other hand, rely on compressed air to generate gripping force, making them suitable for high-speed operations and handling heavier items. Both types are essential in industries such as manufacturing, logistics, and food processing, enhancing automation and efficiency.</p></p>
