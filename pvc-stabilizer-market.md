<p><p>The PVC stabilizer market is categorized into several types, including lead stabilizers, mixed metal stabilizers, tin stabilizers, and organic stabilizers. Lead stabilizers are traditional and cost-effective but face regulatory challenges due to health concerns. Mixed metal stabilizers, often combining various metals, provide enhanced stability and lower toxicity. Tin stabilizers are considered safer alternatives, offering effective stabilization with less environmental impact. Organic stabilizers, derived from non-toxic materials, are gaining popularity for their eco-friendly profile, appealing to sustainability-focused manufacturers.</p></p>
