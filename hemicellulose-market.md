<p><p>Hemicellulose is a type of complex carbohydrate found in plant cell walls. It can be broken down into different types, including Polyxylose, Polyglucose Mannose, and Polygalactose Grape Mannose. Polyxylose is composed of xylose sugar units, Polyglucose Mannose consists of a mixture of glucose and mannose sugars, and Polygalactose Grape Mannose is made up of galactose and mannose sugars. These different types of hemicellulose have various industrial applications, including being used in the production of biofuels, food additives, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
