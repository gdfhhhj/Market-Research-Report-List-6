<p><p>NanoCrystalline Cellulose (NCC) encompasses three key market types: Nanofibrillated Cellulose (NFC), Nanocrystalline Cellulose, and Bacterial Nanocellulose. NFC is characterized by its long, flexible fibers that enhance mechanical properties, making it suitable for composites and films. Nanocrystalline Cellulose, derived from plant sources, exhibits high strength and thermal stability, ideal for applications in coatings and pharmaceuticals. Bacterial Nanocellulose, produced by certain bacteria, offers unique biocompatibility and is utilized in medical and cosmetic products. Together, they contribute significantly to the sustainable materials market.</p></p>
