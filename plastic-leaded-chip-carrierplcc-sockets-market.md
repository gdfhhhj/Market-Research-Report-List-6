<p><p>Plastic Leaded Chip Carrier (PLCC) sockets are essential components used to securely connect PLCC packages to circuit boards. The market is divided into two primary types: Through-Hole Technology (THT) sockets, which are inserted into drilled holes in the circuit board and soldered from the underside, ensuring a strong mechanical connection; and Surface Mount Technology (SMT) sockets, which are mounted directly on the surface of the board, offering a more compact design and improving assembly efficiency. Both types cater to different application needs.</p></p>
