<p><ul><li>Chemiluminescence Immunoassay</li><li>Time-resolved Immunoassay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Thyroxine Test Kit market includes various types of assays such as Chemiluminescence Immunoassay and Time-resolved Immunoassay. Chemiluminescence Immunoassay utilizes light emissions from chemical reactions to detect thyroxine levels, offering high sensitivity and specificity. Conversely, Time-resolved Immunoassay employs lanthanide chelates, measuring luminescence over time, enhancing detection capability. Both methods are pivotal for assessing thyroid function, guiding diseases like hypothyroidism and hyperthyroidism, and expanding the clinical diagnostics landscape for accurate hormone profiling.</p></p>
