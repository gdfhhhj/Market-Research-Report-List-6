<p><p>Skin biopsy market is segmented based on the types of procedures like shave biopsy, excisional biopsy, and incisional biopsy. Shave biopsy involves shaving off the top layers of the skin for testing, making it less invasive. Excisional biopsy removes entire skin lesions for examination, while incisional biopsy takes a sample from a specific area of a larger skin lesion. These different types cater to various clinical needs and preferences, providing healthcare providers with options for accurate diagnosis and treatment planning.</p></p>
