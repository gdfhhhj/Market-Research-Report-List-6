FRAM combines speed with low power needs through ferroelectric materials. RERAM changes resistance states for data storage, while 3D Xpoint merges memory and storage benefits. Nano RAM offers ultra-fast access. Other technologies continue to emerge, enhancing data retention and performance.</p></p>
