<p><p>The external storage market comprises three primary types: magnetic storage, optical storage, and flash memory devices. Optical storage includes CDs, DVDs, and Blu-ray discs, known for their durability and portability but limited capacity. Flash memory devices, like USB drives and solid-state drives (SSDs), provide fast access speeds and decreasing prices, making them popular for everyday data transfer and storage needs.</p></p>
