<p><p>The commercial control damper market encompasses various applications designed for specific environmental regulations and safety measures. Smoke dampers are utilized to prevent smoke movement within air ducts during a fire, ensuring safe evacuation. Fire dampers, on the other hand, automatically close to block flames from spreading through duct systems, maintaining compartmentalization. Other dampers include volume control and outdoor air dampers that regulate airflow and maintain indoor air quality.
