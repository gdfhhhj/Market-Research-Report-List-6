<p><p>The thermofusible film market comprises various types, each offering unique properties. TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethanes) provides flexibility and durability, making it suitable for applications like medical and automotive sectors. EVA (Ethylene Vinyl Acetate) is valued for its clarity and adhesion, commonly used in packaging. PA (Polyamide) films offer strength and chemical resistance, ideal for industrial uses. PES (Polyethersulfone) boasts high thermal stability, while PO (Polyolefin) films are lightweight and versatile, often used in protective and packaging applications. Each material caters to specific industry needs, enhancing product performance.</p></p>
