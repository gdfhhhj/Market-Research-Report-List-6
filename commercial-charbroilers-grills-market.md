<p><p>The commercial charbroilers and grills market can be categorized into three main types: electric charbroilers, gas charbroilers, and charcoal charbroilers. Electric charbroilers utilize electricity for heating, offering precise temperature control and convenience, making them ideal for indoor settings. Gas charbroilers run on natural gas or propane, providing high heat output and quick cooking times, favored in busy kitchens. Charcoal charbroilers, known for imparting a smoky flavor, are often used in outdoor grilling environments, appealing to traditionalists and barbecue enthusiasts.</p></p>
