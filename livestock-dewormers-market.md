<p><p>Livestock dewormers are available in three main types: topical, injection, and oral. Topical dewormers are applied externally to the skin or fur of the animal. Injection dewormers are administered directly into the animal's bloodstream. Oral dewormers are given to the animal via the mouth, usually in the form of a pill or liquid. Each type of dewormer has its own advantages and is chosen based on factors such as the type of parasite being targeted and the ease of administration for the animal.</p></p>
