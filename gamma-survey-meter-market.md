<p><ul><li>Geiger Counter Type</li><li>Ion Chamber Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gamma survey meters are essential devices for measuring ionizing radiation, primarily used in environmental monitoring and radiation safety. The Geiger counter type is popular for its affordability and ease of use, effectively detecting gamma radiation through gas-filled tubes. In contrast, the ion chamber type is more suited for precise measurements and higher radiation levels, utilizing air-filled chambers to measure ionization directly. Together, these market segments cater to a wide range of applications in nuclear power, medical facilities, and research laboratories.</p></p>
