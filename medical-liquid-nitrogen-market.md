<p><p>The medical liquid nitrogen market is primarily categorized into two types: on-site gas systems and bottled gas. On-site gas systems involve generating liquid nitrogen directly at the facility, offering a continuous supply and cost-effectiveness for high-demand users. In contrast, bottled gas involves purchasing pre-filled cylinders, providing flexibility and easier access for facilities with lower usage. Both types serve critical medical applications, including cryopreservation, tissue freezing, and various surgical procedures, ensuring the safe handling and storage of biological materials.</p></p>
