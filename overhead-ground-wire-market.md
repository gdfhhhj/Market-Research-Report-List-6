<p><strong>The Overhead Ground Wire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Central Tube Structure</li><li>Layer Stranding Structure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The overhead ground wire market is divided into two primary types: central tube structure and layer stranding structure. The central tube structure consists of a single central conductor surrounded by multiple wire layers, offering enhanced electrical performance and structural integrity. In contrast, the layer stranding structure features multiple strands of conductor wires twisted together, providing flexibility and ease of installation. Both types serve to protect transmission lines from lightning strikes and reduce electromagnetic interference, playing a crucial role in electrical infrastructure.</p></p>
