<p><p>The acute care ventilator market features three primary types: electronic, pneumatic, and electro-pneumatic ventilators. Electronic ventilators use digital technology for precise control and monitoring, offering advanced functionalities. Pneumatic ventilators rely on compressed air mechanisms, making them simple and reliable, particularly in emergency settings. Electro-pneumatic ventilators combine features of both, utilizing electronic controls to enhance pneumatic systems, allowing for more versatile patient management. Each type plays a crucial role in delivering respiratory support in critical care environments.</p></p>
