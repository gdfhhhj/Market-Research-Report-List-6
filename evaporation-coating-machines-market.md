<p><p>Evaporation coating machines are used in different types of markets, such as PVD (Physical Vapor Deposition) and CVD (Chemical Vapor Deposition). PVD involves the deposition of a thin film coating onto a substrate through the process of physical vaporization, while CVD involves the chemical reactions of precursor gases to form a coating on the substrate. Both types of coating technologies are used in various industries like electronics, automotive, aerospace, and medical devices to enhance the surface properties of materials for improved performance and durability.</p></p>
