<p><p>The railway coupling market is categorized into three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and automatic couplings. Manual couplings require human intervention for connection and disconnection, emphasizing operator skill and safety. Semi-automatic couplings streamline the process by integrating some automation, reducing manual effort while still needing operator assistance. Automatic couplings offer the highest efficiency by enabling trains to connect and disconnect autonomously, enhancing operational speed and safety, and significantly decreasing turnaround times in rail operations.</p></p>
