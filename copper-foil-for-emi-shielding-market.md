<p><p>The Copper Foil for EMI Shielding market primarily features two types: Rolled Copper Foil and Electrolytic Copper Foil. Rolled Copper Foil is produced through mechanical processes and offers good flexibility, making it suitable for various applications, including printed circuit boards and flexible electronics. In contrast, Electrolytic Copper Foil is created using an electroplating method, resulting in a thinner and more uniform foil, ideal for high-frequency applications due to its superior conductivity and improved shielding effectiveness against electromagnetic interference (EMI).</p></p>
