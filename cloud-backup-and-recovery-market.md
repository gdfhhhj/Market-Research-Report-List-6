<p><p>Cloud backup and recovery market can be classified into three main types: private cloud, public cloud, and hybrid cloud. </p><p>In private cloud, backup and recovery solutions are hosted and managed within an organization's own data center, providing control and security but requiring upfront investment. Public cloud solutions are provided by third-party vendors, offering scalability and cost-effectiveness but with potential security concerns. </p><p>Hybrid cloud combines elements of both private and public cloud, allowing organizations to keep sensitive data on-premises while leveraging the flexibility and scalability of the public cloud.</p></p>
