<p><ul><li>Roller Peristaltic Pump</li><li>Shoe type Peristaltic Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Peristaltic pumps are essential in mining and mineral extraction, delivering reliable and efficient fluid transfer. The roller peristaltic pump utilizes multiple rollers to compress a flexible tube, creating a suction effect that moves fluids with minimal shear stress, ideal for abrasive materials. In contrast, shoe-type peristaltic pumps use a shoe that presses against the tube, providing a gentler method for handling sensitive fluids. Both types ensure consistent flow rates and reduce the risk of contamination, making them suitable for harsh mining environments.</p></p>
