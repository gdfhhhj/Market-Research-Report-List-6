<p><p>The lock washers market comprises several types, including external and internal variants, star washers, split washers, and others. External lock washers grip the surface of the assembly, preventing loosening, while internal lock washers fit into the bolt’s head for added security. Star washers feature prongs that dig into surfaces to resist turning. Split washers, or spring washers, provide a spring-like effect to maintain tension. These types cater to diverse applications across industries, ensuring reliability in fastening solutions.</p></p>
