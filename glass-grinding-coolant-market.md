<p><ul><li>Synthetic Type</li><li>Semi Synthetic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glass grinding coolant market is primarily divided into two types: synthetic and semi-synthetic coolants. Synthetic coolants are chemically formulated to provide superior cooling and lubrication properties, enhancing grinding efficiency and extending tool life. They often contain no oil, reducing residue and environmental impact. In contrast, semi-synthetic coolants blend synthetic components with mineral oils, offering a balance of performance and cost-effectiveness. These coolants provide effective cooling while maintaining some lubrication characteristics, making them versatile for various grinding applications.</p></p>
