<p><p>The artificial heart-lung machine market is primarily categorized into two types: single roller pump heart-lung machines (HLM) and double roller pump HLM. Single roller pump HLMs utilize a single roller mechanism for blood circulation, offering simplicity and ease of use, often preferred in less complex procedures. In contrast, double roller pump HLMs employ two rollers, providing enhanced flow dynamics and stability, making them suitable for more intricate surgical interventions. Both types are crucial for cardiac surgeries requiring cardiopulmonary bypass support.</p></p>
