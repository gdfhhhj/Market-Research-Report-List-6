<p><p>The brake oil aftermarket for off-highway vehicles is segmented primarily into four types: DOT 3, DOT 4, DOT 5, and DOT 5.1. DOT 3 is a glycol-based fluid suitable for light-duty applications, while DOT 4 offers higher boiling points for more intense conditions. DOT 5 is silicone-based and not compatible with glycol fluids, ideal for vehicles requiring non-corrosive properties. Finally, DOT 5.1 combines high performance with glycol compatibility, making it suited for various off-highway demands.</p></p>
