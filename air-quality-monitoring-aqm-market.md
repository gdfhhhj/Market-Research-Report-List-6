<p><p>Air Quality Monitoring (AQM) involves the systematic measurement of pollutants and other airborne particles to assess the quality of air in various environments. The AQM market is divided into two main types: indoor monitors and outdoor monitors. Indoor monitors focus on air quality within enclosed spaces, tracking pollutants like volatile organic compounds, carbon dioxide, and particulate matter. Outdoor monitors assess air quality in the environment, measuring pollutants from sources like traffic and industrial activities, providing data crucial for public health and regulatory compliance.</p></p>
