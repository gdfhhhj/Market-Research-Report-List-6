<p><p>The medical grade silicone tubing and hose market is categorized primarily into platinum-cured and peroxide-cured types. Platinum-cured silicone is known for its superior purity, biocompatibility, and resistance to temperature extremes, making it suitable for long-term medical applications. In contrast, peroxide-cured silicone is typically more economical, providing good mechanical properties and flexibility but may contain by-products that can affect biocompatibility. Both types cater to a range of medical applications, from fluid delivery systems to surgical devices, ensuring safety and performance.</p></p>
