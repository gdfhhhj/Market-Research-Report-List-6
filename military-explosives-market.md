<p><p>The military explosives market is categorized into three main types: liquid, solid, and gel explosives. Liquid explosives are comprised of liquid compounds that can be easily transported and mixed before use, offering flexibility in deployment. Solid explosives are typically in a stable, solid form, providing reliability and ease of handling, commonly used in munitions and ordnance. Gel explosives combine properties of both, offering high stability and versatility, often used in specialized applications requiring precise delivery and performance under various conditions.</p></p>
