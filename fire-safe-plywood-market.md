<p><ul><li>UCFA</li><li>UCFB</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fire Safe Plywood market is distinctly categorized into two main types: UCFA (Unfinished Combustible Fire-resistant Attributes) and UCFB (Unfinished Combustible Fire-retardant Blocks). UCFA refers to plywood products that possess inherent fire-resistant features without additional treatments, making them ideal for areas requiring basic fire safety standards. UCFB, on the other hand, includes plywood treated with fire-retardant chemicals, enhancing its ability to resist flames and heat. Both types cater to varying building codes and safety regulations in construction.</p></p>
