<p><p>Logging trailers are available with different market types such as those equipped with Anti-Lock Brakes (ABS) or Electronic Brake System (EBS). These systems prevent wheel lock-up during braking, enhancing safety on the road. Load Proportioning Brake Systems are also common in logging trailers, which adjust the braking force according to the weight distribution of the load. This helps to evenly distribute the braking power among all wheels, improving stability and control while driving, especially when carrying heavy loads on uneven terrain.</p></p>
