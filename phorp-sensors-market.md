<p><p>The pH/ORP sensors market includes two primary types: Glass Type Sensors and ISFET Sensors. Glass Type Sensors utilize a glass membrane to measure hydrogen ion concentration, offering high accuracy and stability in various environments. They are widely used in laboratories and industrial applications. Conversely, ISFET (Ion-Sensitive Field-Effect Transistor) Sensors utilize semiconductor technology to measure pH levels, providing faster response times and greater durability. ISFET Sensors are increasingly favored in portable and miniaturized devices, enhancing their versatility across diverse applications.</p></p>
