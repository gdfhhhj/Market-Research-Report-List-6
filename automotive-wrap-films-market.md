<p><strong>The Automotive Wrap Films Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cast Film</li><li>Calendered Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive wrap films are commonly divided into two types: cast film and calendered film. Cast film is made using a method where liquid resin is spread onto a carrier sheet and then cured through a baking process, resulting in a thin and flexible film with high conformability. On the other hand, calendered film is manufactured by feeding heated PVC through a series of rollers to create a thicker and less flexible film. Both types of films have their own advantages and are used for various applications in the automotive industry.</p></p>
