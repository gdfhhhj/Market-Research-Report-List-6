<p><p>High-frequency welding diamond saw blades are categorized into two main market types based on operational methods: dry operation and wet operation. Dry operation blades are designed for use without water, utilizing high-frequency welding techniques to enhance durability and performance in cutting hard materials like concrete and stone. In contrast, wet operation blades require water to cool the cutting edge, reduce dust, and improve cutting efficiency, making them suitable for applications requiring smooth, precise cuts and extended blade life.</p></p>
