<p><p>Critical limb ischemia (CLI) treatment market includes various types of therapies such as embolic protection devices, peripheral dilatation systems, antiplatelet drugs, and antihypertensive agents. Embolic protection devices are used to prevent debris from entering the bloodstream during peripheral angioplasty. Peripheral dilatation systems are devices used to open blocked arteries in the limbs. Antiplatelet drugs help prevent blood clots, while antihypertensive agents help lower blood pressure. These treatments aim to improve blood flow and reduce the risk of amputation in patients with CLI.</p></p>
