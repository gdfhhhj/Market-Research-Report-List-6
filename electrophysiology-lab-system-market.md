<p><p>The electrophysiology lab system market includes several specialized technologies. X-ray systems are used for imaging during procedures, while 3D mapping systems provide detailed anatomical representations for accurate navigation. EP recording systems capture and analyze electrical signals from the heart. Remote steering systems allow for precise manipulation of catheters. Intracardiac echocardiography systems offer real-time ultrasound imaging inside the heart, and radiofrequency (RF) ablation generators deliver energy to target and destroy cardiac tissue abnormalities, enhancing treatment efficacy.</p></p>
