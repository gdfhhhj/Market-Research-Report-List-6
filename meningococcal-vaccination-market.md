<p><p>Meningococcal vaccination market includes three main types - polysaccharide, conjugate, and combination vaccines. Polysaccharide vaccines stimulate the body's immune response to protect against meningitis caused by meningococcal bacteria. Conjugate vaccines are more effective, especially in young children, as they create a stronger immune response. Combination vaccines protect against multiple strains of meningococcal bacteria in a single shot. Each type offers different levels of protection and is used based on age, risk factors, and specific strains of the bacteria.</p></p>
