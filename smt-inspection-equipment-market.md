<p><strong>The SMT Inspection Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AOI</li><li>SPI</li><li>AXI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SMT inspection equipment market comprises three primary types: Automated Optical Inspection (AOI), Solder Paste Inspection (SPI), and Automated X-ray Inspection (AXI). AOI uses high-resolution cameras to detect surface defects on assembled PCBs, ensuring quality control. SPI measures the thickness and volume of solder paste to optimize printing processes. AXI examines hidden connections and internal structures by using X-ray technology, crucial for detecting defects in multilayer boards. Together, these technologies enhance the reliability and performance of electronic assemblies.</p></p>
