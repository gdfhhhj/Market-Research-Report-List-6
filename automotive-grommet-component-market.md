<p><p>The automotive grommet component market is categorized into three main types based on material: EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer), NR (Natural Rubber), and SBR (Styrene-Butadiene Rubber). EPDM is known for its excellent weather resistance and durability, making it ideal for outdoor applications. NR provides high elasticity and tensile strength, suitable for various automotive parts. SBR offers good abrasion resistance and cost-effectiveness, commonly used in various automotive components. Each material type caters to specific performance and durability requirements in vehicles.</p></p>
